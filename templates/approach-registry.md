## Status Definitions

- `active`: investigation is underway
- `promising`: concrete mechanism and supporting evidence exist
- `blocked`: requires an unproved or unavailable load-bearing assumption
- `refuted`: a valid counterexample defeats the approach within scope
- `survived`: no current refuter defeated the approach within scope
- `deferred`: intentionally outside the current budget or authority
